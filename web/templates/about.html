About Mindloop

Mindloop is designed to bring clarity to your chaotic digital life. By simplifying how you track your habits, focus, and intentions, we help you close the loops in your mind.

Philosophy

We believe in "Serene Focus". Tools should not demand your attention; they should help you direct it. Mindloop is built to be minimal, calm, and effective.

Methodology

Combine daily intention setting, time-boxed focus sessions, and consistent habit tracking to build a feedback loop of positive reinforcement.

Built with Go on the backend and pure HTML/CSS on the frontend.
